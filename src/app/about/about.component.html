who am i

I jumped into the programming world back in 2020, and since then, it's been a whirlwind of learning and experimenting. I'm based in Rabat, Morocco, soaking up all the tech vibes in this vibrant city. Over time, I've dabbled in various languages and technologies, trying to find my niche. I've tinkered with Java, Spring Boot, PHP, JavaScript, Node.js, Nest.js, TypeScript, and more. I'm passionate about building seamless experiences for users, whether it's through responsive web designs or robust backend systems. And hey, I'm always eager to learn and grow, because in this ever-evolving field, there's always something new to explore.
